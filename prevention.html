Prevention

Now that you know how much and where gun violence happens, its time to take action. We need to figure out how to prevent it from happening. But before we are able to find out how to stop it, we need to find the main causes of it. There are a plethora of different causes, but below, I have divided them into three main categories. From there, we will dive in to the prevention step, and find ways to prevent the violence from happening.

Poverty & Economic Inequality

Cause #1: Poverty & Economic Inequality

Poverty is a big cause of gun violence. Almost 25% of people in philadelphia live in poverty, leading to hopelessness and desperation. Poverty often restricts individuals access to employment. This creates an open area for high stress and this could lead to desperation. Poverty also means a lack of resources and affordable healthcare. Without these resources, people could feel marginalized or disconnected from the rest of society, leading to social unrest and/or illegal activites.

1/2

Ways to Prevent:

Preventing poverty in Philadelphia necessitates a multi-faceted approach. Investing in education and vocational training programs can equip individuals with the skills needed for stable employment, thus reducing unemployment rates. Implementing policies that promote affordable housing and healthcare access can alleviate financial burdens on low-income families. Furthermore, supporting small businesses and fostering economic development in underserved communities can create job opportunities and stimulate local economies. Lastly, strengthening social safety nets and providing targeted assistance to vulnerable populations can help mitigate the impacts of poverty and promote upward mobility.
