Learn Where

You know how much gun violence happens. You have the general statistics. But to get detailed statistics we need to dive deeper into philaldephia. Instead of looking at how much gun violence happens in Philadelphia as a whole, we need to know how much it happens in certain areas of Philly. By doing this, we will get a detailed view of Gun Violence in Philly.

Overview of the Maps

North Philadelphia's gun violence situation is a deeply entrenched issue that continues to afflict the community. High levels of crime, fueled by socioeconomic challenges, gang activity, and the drug trade, contribute to a cycle of violence and a pervasive sense of insecurity. The impact on families and the community as a whole is devastating, with lives lost and lasting trauma endured.
